PHQ-4 — Psychological Distress Screening (Intake)
Rate each item for the past 2 weeks. Ask the client in their language and record their answer. 0 = Not at all   1 = Several days   2 = More than half the days   3 = Nearly every day.
1. Feeling nervous, anxious, or on edge
2. Not being able to stop or control worrying
3. Feeling down, depressed, or hopeless
4. Little interest or pleasure in doing things
